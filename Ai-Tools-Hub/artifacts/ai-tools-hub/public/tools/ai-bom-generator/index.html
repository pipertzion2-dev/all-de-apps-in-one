FAQ

What is the AI BOM Generator Playground?

The playground is a free, no-login tool that runs one isolated step of Svivva’s real pipeline. You submit a short brief and get a production-style preview (e.g. BOM table, idea report, or flow) so you can see what the full workflow offers before signing up.

Is the AI BOM Generator Playground really free?

Yes. You can use it online without creating an account. The preview is a single step; the full workflow with versioning, collaboration, and exports is available inside Svivva (free trial available).

How does this map to Svivva?

Each mode (Hardware, Play, Idea, Team, Marketplace, Build) corresponds to a specific part of Svivva. The right panel and the preview explanation show which step you’re seeing and what additional steps exist in the full platform.
