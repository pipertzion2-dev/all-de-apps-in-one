How the Free Online Build Planner Works

The Svivva build planner is a free online tool designed for hardware engineers, product designers, musicians, and creative teams who need a fast way to validate concepts before committing to a full development cycle. Instead of spending hours configuring toolchains or setting up project scaffolding, you describe your concept in plain language, select the workflow category that matches your project, and receive a production-grade preview in seconds.

Supported Workflow Categories

The build planner covers six distinct workflow categories, each mapping to a real step in the Svivva production pipeline:

  • Hardware — Generates a schematic diagram and bill of materials (BOM) table from your concept description. Useful for electronics prototyping, circuit design, and hardware startups evaluating component choices early.
  • Play — Produces a performance setup map and audio waveform preview. Built for musicians, sound designers, and live performers working with routing, stems, and signal chains.
  • Idea — Returns a feasibility score and structured idea report. Ideal for product managers and founders who need quick validation before deeper research or team alignment.
  • Team — Maps a collaboration flow showing roles, permissions, and shared asset handoffs. Designed for distributed teams planning how work moves between contributors.
  • Marketplace — Checks publish readiness with a structured checklist covering scope, documentation, and packaging requirements. Useful for anyone preparing an API, module, or component for distribution.
  • Build — Generates a 3D placeholder preview representing the digital-to-physical production flow. Covers build steps, materials, costing, and export preparation.
