How the Chord Progression Generator Works

Most chord generators give you random output or force you to click through music theory menus. This one works differently. You describe what you want in your own words — something like "upbeat pop chorus," "moody minor progression for a product demo," or "jazzy four-bar loop" — and the generator analyzes your description to select a chord palette that fits your intent.

You can also specify a genre (pop, jazz, electronic, and more) and a mode to shape the output further. The tool evaluates your brief and returns a chord progression along with a readiness score, so you know how well your description mapped to a usable result. The entire process takes less than a second.
