What is an AI BOM Generator?

A bill of materials (BOM) is the backbone of every hardware project. It lists every component—resistors, capacitors, microcontrollers, connectors, PCBs—along with values, package sizes, quantities, and estimated costs. Traditionally, engineers build BOMs by hand in spreadsheets after finishing a schematic, a process that takes hours, invites copy-paste errors, and falls out of sync the moment a design changes.

An AI BOM generator flips that workflow. Instead of waiting until the schematic is complete, you describe your project in plain language—"compact MIDI controller with 8 rotary encoders, USB-C power, open-source firmware"—and the tool produces a preliminary BOM in seconds. The result is not a final production file, but it gives you an immediate sense of component count, cost envelope, and feasibility before you invest time in detailed design.

How the Svivva AI BOM Generator works
