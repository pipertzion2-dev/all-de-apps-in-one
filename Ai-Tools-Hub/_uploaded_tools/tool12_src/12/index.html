How to read this

Use these dimensions to improve your prompt before shipping to production.

Clarity — Is the intent unambiguous? Clear instructions raise confidence.
Specificity — Concrete examples and constraints improve reliability.
Structure — Sections, bullets, and formatting help the model follow the prompt.
Safety & guardrails — Boundaries and refusal instructions reduce harmful outputs.
Output shape — Defined format (JSON, list, length) increases predictability.

Low scores? Refine that dimension and re-run. In production, use schema enforcement and monitoring.
