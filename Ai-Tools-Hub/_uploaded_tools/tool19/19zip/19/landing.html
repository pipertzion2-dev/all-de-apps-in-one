What is a bill of materials generator?

A bill of materials (BOM) generator takes your product concept or hardware description and outputs a structured list of components, materials, and specifications needed to build it. This free BOM generator from Svivva lets you describe your concept in plain language and instantly returns a production-style material match preview — no spreadsheet required.

Yes. The BOM preview tool is completely free and requires no login or signup. You can generate as many previews as you need. For full BOM exports, cost tracking, version history, and team collaboration, you can upgrade to the full Svivva platform.

What types of projects can I generate a BOM for?

You can generate BOM previews for hardware products (synthesizers, enclosures, PCB assemblies, electronics), physical product prototypes, musical instrument builds, IoT devices, and digital/physical hybrid builds.
