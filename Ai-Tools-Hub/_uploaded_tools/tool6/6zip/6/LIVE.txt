GET A LIVE URL (so you can see the app in any browser)
======================================================

1. Double-click:  Make Live.command

2. A browser tab will open (Netlify Drop) and this folder will open in Finder.

3. Drag the file  deploy.zip  from this folder onto the Netlify web page.

4. Netlify will give you a URL like:  https://something-random.netlify.app
   That is your live app. Open it, bookmark it, share it. No signup required.

Done. The app is live.
